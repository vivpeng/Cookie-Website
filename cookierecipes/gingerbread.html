GINGERBREAD COOKIES RECIPE

Ingredients

unsalted butter 1 cup 250 mL
granulated sugar 1 cup 250 mL
molasses 1 cup 250 mL
brown sugar 1/2 cup 125 mL
egg 1 egg n/a
all-purpose flour 4 cups 1 L
baking soda 1 tsp 5 mL
ground ginger 1/2 tsp 2 mL
ground clove 1/2 tsp 2 mL
plain or flavoured yogurt 3/4 cup 175 mL
vanilla extract 1/2 tsp 2 mL
pinch of salt n/a n/a

Let’s Make

  1. In a large bowl, cream butter. Stir in sugar, molasses, and brown sugar. Add eggs and beat until mixture is fluffy.
  2. Sift together flour, salt, baking soda, and spices. Add to the first mixture and blend. Stir in yogurt and vanilla extract.
  3. Shape dough into a large ball and chill for at least 12 hours in the fridge.

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Grease a cookie sheet.
  2. Lightly flour a board and sprinkle it with sugar. Roll dough. Shape cookies with a cookie cutter and transfer to a cookie sheet.
  3. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes.
