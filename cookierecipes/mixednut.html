MIXED-NUT COOKIES RECIPE

Ingredients

unsalted butter 1 cup 250 mL
brown sugar 2 cups 500 mL
eggs, beaten 2 eggs n/a
all-purpose flour 3 1/2 cups 875 mL
baking soda 1 tsp 5 mL
chopped nuts 1 cup 250 mL
pinch of salt n/a n/a

Let’s Make

  1. In a large bowl, cream butter with brown sugar. Add eggs and mix well.
  2. In another bowl, mix together flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually blend into batter. Mix in nuts.
  3. Divide dough in half and shape into 2 logs/blocks. Wrap in waxed paper and secure in foil.
  4. Refrigerate at least 12 hours before baking.

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Grease and flour a cookie sheet.
  2. Cut dough into 1/4 inch (5 mm) thick slices and arrange on a sheet.
  3. Bake for 8-10 minutes.

*NOTE: this cookie's cookie dough will keep up to 5 days in the refrigerator.
