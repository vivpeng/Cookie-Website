COCOA BUTTONS RECIPE

Ingredients

vegetable shortening 1/3 cup 75 mL
packed brown sugar 3/4 cup 175 mL
egg 1 egg n/a
instant coffee crystals 1 tbsp 15 mL
cocoa powder 2 tbsp 30 mL
all-purpose flour 2 tbsp 30 mL

Let’s Make

  1. In a large bowl, cream shortening with brown sugar. Add egg and beat well.
  2. Dissolve instant coffee in hot water and add to the mixture. Add cocoa, then gradually add flour until well-mixed
  3. Divide dough in two and shape each into a log about 2 inches (5 cm) in diameter. Wrap in plastic and refrigerate.

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Lightly grease cookie sheets.
  2. Slice dough into 1/4 inch (5 mm) slices and place on cookie sheets.
  3. Using a plastic straw, press 2 or 4 holes into each circle
  4. Bake for 8-10 minutes.

*NOTE: make sure to store the cookies in an airtight container.
