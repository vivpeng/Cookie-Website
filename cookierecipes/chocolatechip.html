CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES RECIPE

Ingredients

unsalted butter 1 cup 250 mL
brown sugar 3/4 cups 175 mL
granulated sugar 3/4 cup 175 mL
eggs 2 eggs n/a
all-purpose flour 2 eggs 500 mL
salt 1/2 tsps 2 mL
baking soda 1 tsp 5 mL
chocolate chips 3/4 cups 175 mL
almond extract 1/4 tsps 1 mL

Let’s Make

  1. In a large bowl, cream butter with both sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs and continue beating for a minute.
  2. Combine dry ingredients and gradually add to batter, beating at a low speed. Mix in chocolate chips and almond extract.

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit. Grease and flour a cookie sheet.
  2. Drop small spoonfuls of batter onto the cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes.
