VANILLA SNAPS RECIPE

Ingredients

unsalted butter, softened 1/4 cup 50 mL
granulated sugar 1/4 cup 50 mL
brown sugar 1/4 cup 50 mL
egg 1 egg n/a
vanilla extract 1/4 tsp 1 mL
all-purpose flour 1 cup 250 mL
baking soda 1/2 tsp 2 mL
pinch of salt n/a n/a

Let’s Make

  1. In a bowl, cream together butter and both sugars. Add egg and vanilla. Mix well.
  2. Sift together dry ingredients and stir into the first mixture.
  3. Shape dough into a 2 inch (5 cm) square log. Should be about 22 inches (55 cm) long.
  4. Chill for 12 hours in refrigerator before baking

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit. Grease a cookie sheet
  2. Cut dough into 1/4 inch (5 mm) thick slices. Transfer to a cookie sheet.
  3. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
