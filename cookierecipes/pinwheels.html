RASPBERRY PINWHEELS RECIPE

Ingredients

unsalted butter 1/2 cup 125 mL
cream cheese 4 oz 125 g
granulated sugar 1 cup 250 mL
egg 1 egg n/a
lemon juice 1 tsp 5 mL
chopped lemon zest 1 tbsp 15 mL
all-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups 550 mL
baking soda 1 tsp 2 mL
raspberry jam 1 cup 250 mL

Let’s Make

  1. In a large bowl, cream butter and cream cheese with sugar, Add egg, juice and lemon zest. Continue beating until light and fluffy.
  2. Sift flour and baking soda together. Gradually add to the creamed mixture, blending after each addition. Chill for an hour.

Let’s Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Lightly grease cookie sheets.
  2. Divide dough in three. Working on a floured board, roll one portion into a rectangle. It should be about 9 x 10 inches (23 x 25 cm) and 1/8 inch (3 mm) thick. Trim the edges. Brush with 1/2 of the raspberry jam and roll up. Repeat with the other two.
  3. Cut rolls into slices and place on cookie sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
